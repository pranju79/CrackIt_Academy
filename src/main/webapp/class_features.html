Class Features

Physical Space

A designated area with desks, chairs, and other educational resources where students and teachers gather for learning.

Seating Arrangement

Organized seating arrangements that facilitate communication, interaction, and visibility among students and with the teacher.

Teaching Tools

Whiteboards, chalkboards, projectors, screens, or other tools used by educators to present information and engage students in the learning process.

Learning Materials

Textbooks, notebooks, pens, and other materials necessary for students to participate in class activities and assignments.

Rules and Expectations:

Clearly defined rules and expectations that help establish a structured and respectful learning environment for both students and teachers.

Interactive Spaces

Areas for group discussions, collaborative projects, or hands-on activities that encourage student engagement and participation.

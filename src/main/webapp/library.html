Agricultural Books

The Natural Way of Farming

In this book Fukuoka explains his green philosophy of farming. He farms without cultivation, (chemical) fertiizers or compost and weeding. After he has explained the theory he describes the practice of the natural production of grains, fruits and vegetables

Handbook of Agriculture: Facts and Figures for Farmers, Students,

Weather and crops. Soils. Water resources. Land utilization. Soil and water conservation. Water management in crop production. Soil fertility and fertilizer use. Cropping patterns. Weed control. Diseases of crops. Insect pests of crops. Nematodes. Agricultural engineering.

The One-Straw Revolution: An Introduction to Natural Farming

Call it “Zen and the Art of Farming” or a “Little Green Book,” Masanobu Fukuoka’s manifesto about farming, eating, and the limits of human knowledge presents a radical challenge to the global systems we rely on for our food.

Geography Books

Geography of India by Majid Husain

Majid Husain’s book “Geography of India” is a comprehensive book on the subject which is very popular amongst students preparing for various competitive examinations. The book covers almost all the topics of Indian Geography

World Geography by Majid Husain

Various geographical regions of the world have been discussed in eighteen chapters with detailed text on individual nations within each of these regions. The Author Majid Husain is former Professor of Geography,Jamia Millia Islamia (Central University), New Delhi.

Economics Books

Arihant PULSE INDIAN ECONOMY Coloured Handwritten Notes

Arihant introduces the first-of-its-kind and meticulously crafted handwritten notes titled as “Pulse”, as a true replacement of traditional bulky textbooks for civil service aspirants. This series is an absolute tool for aspirants to excel in the UPSC exams, providing perfect revision of concepts in a concise manner.

General Studies Paper 1 Prelim & Main Exams | Modern History

TOPPER’S NOTES' is a compilation of meticulously crafted handwritten notes by Aniruddh Yadav, who secured the 8th All India Rank in the UPSC Civil Services Examination in 2022. This book is a valuable resource for General Studies Paper I for UPSC (CSE) aspirants, offering essential content linkages for both Preliminary and Mains Examination. This book encompasses 4 broad subjects Modern History, Geography, Art & Culture and Indian Society as per the syllabus provided by UPSC. What sets it apart from other books is the integrated and interdisciplinary approach that connects concepts across the General Studies syllabus.

11 Year-wise UPSC Civil Services IAS Mains Essay Previous Year Solved Papers (2013 - 2023)

The thoroughly updated 5th Edition of 11 Years UPSC Civil Services IAS Mains ESSAY Year-wise Solved (2013 - 2023) consists of Past 11 years Solved Papers of the IAS Mains Paper 1 - Essay. The USP of this book is that the Word Limit for each solution is strictly adhered as per the UPSC requirements. The book covers the 84 Essays asked in the IAS Mains Paper 1 (2013 - 2023). Each of the Essays is classified into one of the 9 Units pertinent to IAS Exams.
